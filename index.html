Full Business Plan • April 2026

Two products. One customer. One funnel.
A wholesale supply business + an AI-powered business operating system for beauty professionals. Dropship model, no inventory. Cold email → podcast → wholesale → software → recurring residual.

Part 2

The 11 Sub-Niches

All serve the same customer, all feed into each other, all have B2B + B2C angles, all 200–700% margins, all dropship-friendly, no seller license required.

1. Microneedling Cartridges

Single-use pen cartridges + PDRN/peptide serums. Wholesale $0.50–$1.50, sell $3–$5. Every treatment = new cartridge.
350%+ margin

2. LED Light Therapy

7-color masks, red light panels, wands. Consumer masks $25–$45 cost, sell $99–$199. Iron Man mask = TikTok viral.
300–400% margin

3. Hydrofacial Machines

Aqua peel machines + AS1/SA2/AO3 solutions. Real HydraFacial = $20K. Yours = $500–$2,500 wholesale.
500%+ on solutions

4. RF Gels & Accessories

Conductive gel every RF/cavitation treatment REQUIRES. $2–$4 cost, sell $18–$28. Impossible to skip.
500–700% margin

5. PDRN Serums

#1 trending ingredient (340% YoY growth). “Salmon DNA facial” viral. $4–$10 cost, sell $35–$58.
400–500% margin

6. Dermaplaning Blades

Sterile single-use blades. 100-pack $15–$22, sell $65–$85. Pro uses 2–5/day. ASMR gold.
300%+ margin

7. Lash Lift & Brow Kits

Fastest-growing service. Kits $10–$18, sell $45–$75. Solutions consumed per treatment.
300–400% margin

8. Head Spa Supplies

THE biggest emerging opportunity. Scalp scrubs $3–$6, sell $24–$38. ASMR TikTok gold. $14.7B market.
500%+ margin

9. Body Contouring

Cavitation, RF, EMS machines. No license required. GLP-1/Ozempic driving 30% sales increase. Highest-revenue category.
100–250% on machines

10. Lymphatic Drainage

Ellie's idea. Dry brushing kits, gua sha, rollers. $3–$8 cost, sell $25–$45. Trending wellness category.
400%+ margin

11. Eyelash Supplies

Via Petra Wholesaling. 1,000 lashes for $340. Sell $15/pack to consumers. Ashley already has this supplier.
4,300% margin

How they all connect: A single treatment session can use products from 5+ sub-niches. Every device sale creates a consumable customer. One esthetician offering facials + lash lifts + dermaplaning + body contouring + head spa buys from ALL categories. These aren't separate businesses — they're one ecosystem.

Partnership Agreement — Term Sheet

Ownership

Justin: 33.33% • Ashley: 33.33% • Ellie: 33.33%
Entity: Florida LLC (Sunbiz.org, $125 filing fee)

Capital

Ashley & Ellie: 100% of cash startup costs ($450–$750).
Justin: $0 cash. Tech platform valued at $3K–$5K.

Revenue Split

Gross − COGS − Marketing − Operating Expenses = Net Profit.
Split 33/33/33. Distributions monthly by the 5th.

Decisions

Day-to-day: area owner decides. Major (>$500, pricing, partnerships): all three agree. Disputes: majority vote (2 of 3).

Intellectual Property

Brand, content, customer list = LLC property.
Justin's tech (Paperclip, COO, Content Multiplier) = Justin's IP, licensed free to LLC.
Beauty Business OS = Justin's separate product, NOT part of LLC. 20% commission for Ashley/Ellie sales.

Exit / Dissolution

60-day written notice. Remaining partners can buy out at fair market value (trailing 6-month avg profit × 12). 12-month non-compete. If all dissolve: liquidate and split 33/33/33.

Expenses

No single expense over $200 without notifying all partners. All business expenses tracked with receipts. Personal expenses never through the business.

Review

Monthly P&L review (all three). Quarterly strategy review. Annual agreement review (is the split still fair? Do roles need adjusting?).
